🎯 Key Interview Points

                    

                        1. Why tumbling windows? Fixed, non-overlapping. Each click in exactly ONE bucket. No double-counting.

                        2. Why dedup before aggregate? At-least-once delivery is easier than exactly-once. Dedup at processor level.

                        3. Why pre-aggregate? Query is O(1) lookup instead of O(N) scan over raw events.

                        4. Why partition by ad_id? All clicks for same ad → same processor. Zero coordination needed.

                        5. Why multiple granularities? Write amplification (3x) but ANY time-range query is fast.

                        6. Why Channel<T>? Bounded = backpressure. Async = non-blocking. Thread-safe = no locks.
